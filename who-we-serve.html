Apartment Buildings & Property Management

Our Primary Market

High-density residential properties are the core of what we do. All Safe Fire Extinguisher Service works with some of the largest property management companies in the United States — managing inspection schedules, service histories, and compliance documentation across large multifamily portfolios throughout Southern California. All Safe handles annual fire extinguisher inspection, recharging, 6-year maintenance, and compliance documentation for apartment buildings of every size — from 4-unit buildings to 500+ unit portfolios across Los Angeles, Orange County, Riverside, and San Diego counties.

Second-Landing Installs: A Specialized Service

Many apartment buildings throughout Southern California were originally constructed without portable fire extinguisher coverage on second-floor landings. All Safe specializes in exactly this installation — assessing each building, selecting the correct equipment and cabinet type for the environment, and installing to code. We have done this across hundreds of properties throughout our service area.

Written Documentation for Compliance Audits

A hanging inspection tag is sufficient for routine compliance. It is not sufficient when an auditor, insurance carrier, or government agency wants a full service record. All Safe provides written service letters documenting what was inspected, what was serviced, and when. Our property management clients rely on this for portfolio-level audits.

Small Businesses

No Account Too Small

Every fire extinguisher in California is subject to the same annual inspection requirements, regardless of how many units a business has. All Safe services single-unit small business accounts with the same thoroughness we bring to large portfolios. We work with restaurants, retail stores, offices, gyms, salons, auto repair shops, and warehouses throughout our 7-county service area.

  • Retail Stores — Storefront and back-of-house coverage, correct unit placement per NFPA 10 travel distance requirements

  • Gyms & Fitness Studios — High-occupancy spaces with straightforward compliance needs
  • Salons & Spas — Small footprint, clear requirements, reliable annual service
  • Warehouses & Light Industrial — Correct coverage for storage density and square footage
