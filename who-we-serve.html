Apartment Buildings & Property Management

Our Primary Market

High-density residential properties are the core of what we do. All Safe works with some of the largest property management companies in the United States — managing inspection schedules, service histories, and compliance documentation across large portfolios of multifamily buildings throughout Southern California.

Second-Landing Installs: A Specialized Service

Many apartment buildings throughout Southern California were originally constructed without portable fire extinguisher coverage on second-floor landings. As code enforcement has tightened and compliance expectations have increased, property owners and managers face the challenge of retroactively installing extinguishers and cabinets on second landings — often on exterior stairwells with no existing infrastructure.

All Safe specializes in exactly this installation. We assess each building, select the correct equipment and cabinet type for the environment, and install to code. We have done this across hundreds of properties throughout our service area.

Written Documentation for Compliance Audits

A hanging inspection tag is sufficient for routine compliance. It is not sufficient when an auditor, insurance carrier, or government agency wants a full service record. All Safe provides written service letters documenting what was inspected, what was serviced, and when. Our property management clients rely on this for portfolio-level audits and compliance reviews.

Small Businesses

No Account Too Small

Every fire extinguisher in California is subject to the same annual inspection requirements, regardless of how many units a business has. All Safe services single-unit small business accounts with the same thoroughness we bring to large portfolios. If you have one extinguisher or one hundred, the compliance standard is identical — and so is our work.

We work with small businesses across a wide range of industries throughout our 7-county service area:

  • Restaurants & Commercial Kitchens — Annual inspection, Class K recharging, and NFPA 96 compliance for kitchen hood systems
  • Retail Stores — Storefront and back-of-house coverage, correct unit placement per NFPA 10 travel distance requirements
  • Small Offices — Professional suites, co-working spaces, and single-tenant offices
  • Auto Repair Shops — Flammable liquid and electrical fire hazards require the right extinguisher types; we assess and recommend correctly
  • Gyms & Fitness Studios — High-occupancy spaces with straightforward compliance needs
  • Salons & Spas — Small footprint, clear requirements, reliable annual service
  • Warehouses & Light Industrial — Correct coverage for storage density and square footage

If your business operates in Southern California and needs fire extinguisher service, call or email us. There is no minimum account size.
