Riverside County is All Safe's home base and primary service area. Temecula and Murrieta are our backyard, and we run regular service routes throughout the Inland Valley corridor — including Menifee, Lake Elsinore, Perris, and Hemet. 🔥 Fire Extinguisher Service — San Bernardino County

Drive time from Temecula: Approximately 45–90 minutes depending on destination.

Cities Served in San Bernardino County

FontanaRancho CucamongaOntarioSan Bernardino RedlandsVictorvilleRialtoColton ChinoChino HillsUplandMontclair

San Bernardino County is one of the most industrially dense regions in the Western United States. The Inland Empire's massive logistics and warehousing corridor — stretching from Ontario through Fontana and into San Bernardino — is home to distribution centers, cold-storage facilities, and manufacturing plants for some of the largest companies in the country. These high-hazard facilities require sophisticated fire extinguisher programs: extra-hazard classifications, higher-rated units, closer placement spacing, and detailed site maps to satisfy local fire authority inspections.

All Safe has extensive experience with the extra-hazard requirements under NFPA 10 that apply to large warehouses and manufacturing facilities. We conduct thorough site assessments, supply the correct extinguisher types and ratings, and maintain service records that satisfy even the most demanding fire marshal inspections in the Inland Empire.

We also serve the growing residential and commercial communities of Chino, Chino Hills, and Rancho Cucamonga, where retail, restaurant, and office development continues at a rapid pace.
