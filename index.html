Why All Safe?

Portable fire extinguishers are all we do. One trade, mastered over 45 years. That singular focus is why the largest property management companies in the country trust us with their portfolios.

Built to Last — Family Owned Since 1981

All Safe was founded by Tom Tortomasi Sr. on June 1, 1981. Tom Sr. remains active. The business has been built across generations and is built to continue that way. The same clients who hired us in 1981 are still clients today.

We Clear the Bar Most Vendors Can’t

Large property management companies, school districts, corporate campuses, and institutional clients don’t just hire a fire extinguisher company — they approve one. That process eliminates most vendors before a single extinguisher is touched. All Safe clears every requirement:

  • Licensed — California OSFM fire extinguisher license types: B, C, D, and F.
  • Bonded & Insured — Fully bonded with commercial general liability coverage meeting the thresholds required by major property management and institutional procurement departments.
  • Bid-Ready — We handle formal bid submissions, vendor onboarding packets, and procurement documentation the way large organizations require.
  • Certificate of Insurance on File — Issued to specification, naming additional insureds as required by your company’s vendor compliance team.
  • Technician Credentials on Record — OSFM license numbers documented per technician, available for vendor file submission.
  • Service Records by Unit — Every inspection performed and documented to code — the standard your auditor will verify against.
  • Multi-Property Scheduling — Consistent, coordinated service across large portfolios without gaps, missed buildings, or scheduling lapses.

If your organization requires a vendor that can survive procurement — not just show up with a toolkit — All Safe is already there.

What We Do

Annual Fire Extinguisher Inspection

OSFM-required annual inspection of all portable fire extinguisher types. Type B, C, D & F licensed.

Fire Extinguisher Recharge & Refilling

Full recharge of depleted or partially discharged extinguishers to factory specification.

Hydrostatic Testing

Required cylinder pressure testing — performed on schedule, not skipped.

Sales & New Construction

Thousands of extinguishers sold direct. New construction installs throughout Southern California.

Apartment Building Installs

Retroactive second-landing installation for buildings originally built without second-floor coverage.

Compliance Documentation

Written service letters for audits — not just a tag on the extinguisher.

6-Year Maintenance

State Fire Marshal-required full internal inspection every 6 years. All Safe tracks service dates so you never fall out of compliance.
