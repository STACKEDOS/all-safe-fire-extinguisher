Common Questions About Fire Extinguisher Service

How often do fire extinguishers need to be inspected in California?

California requires a visual inspection monthly (which can be done by an employee) and a full annual inspection by an OSFM-certified technician. Hydrostatic testing is required every 5–12 years depending on extinguisher type.

What areas do you serve for fire extinguisher service?

We serve all of Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, and surrounding communities in Riverside County, plus San Diego, Orange, San Bernardino, Los Angeles, Ventura, and Imperial counties.

Do you service restaurant hood suppression systems?

Yes. Commercial kitchen hood suppression systems require semi-annual service under NFPA 96. Our certified technicians handle inspection, recharging, and certification for restaurants throughout Southern California.
