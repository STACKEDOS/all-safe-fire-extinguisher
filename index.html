What We Do

Annual Inspection

OSFM-required annual inspection of all portable fire extinguisher types. Type B, C, D & F licensed.

Recharging & Refilling

Full recharge of depleted or partially discharged extinguishers to factory specification.

Hydrostatic Testing

Required cylinder pressure testing — performed on schedule, not skipped.

Sales & New Construction

Thousands of extinguishers sold direct. New construction installs throughout Southern California.

Apartment Building Installs

Retroactive second-landing installation for buildings originally built without second-floor coverage.

Compliance Documentation

Written service letters for audits — not just a tag on the extinguisher.

6-Year Maintenance

State Fire Marshal-required full internal inspection every 6 years. All Safe tracks service dates so you never fall out of compliance.
